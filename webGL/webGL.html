WebGL Graphics and Shading

Date: Fall 2020

Role: Partner

Course: Introduction to Computer Graphics

Skills: WebGL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, 3D Lighting and Shading Techniques

The goal of this project was to combine knowledge of animation of 3D figures and user movements with lighting and shading to create a world including all three using two different types of shading (Gouraurd and Phong) and two different types of lighting (Phong and Blinn-Phong). This is an example of an especially well done final project for the course, and later earned me a position as a Peer Mentor (Undergrad TA) in the course.
