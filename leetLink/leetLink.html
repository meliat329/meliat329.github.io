LeetLink Application

Date: Winter 2021

Role: Lead Group Member

Course: Technology and Human Interaction

Skills: UI/UX Design Skills, User Research, Figma, Human-Computer Interaction

App Idea

My group and I began by pitching our app ideas to each other. My app idea, a way for gamers to connect with other people you play video games, was the one that we chose to design.

Sketch Prototypes

We did a low fidelity prototype on paper at first.

User Testing

We tested our low fidelity prototype with users and found that because we used a Tinder-esque way of matching, we may run into issues with people trying to use our app for dating. This was not something we wanted, but we wanted something that was intuitive for users like the card swiping style of matching that dating apps use. This led us to adding rules that must be agreed to when the user signs up.

Final Prototype

Our final prototype is on Figma and is moderately functional. If we were to continue this we would refine the branding, and continue iterating on the design.
