Three Arts Club Video

Date: Fall 2018

Role: Video Creator

Course: Ancient Rome in Chicago

Skills: Historical Research, Classical Architecture, Video Filming and Editing

This video project was done to be added to a larger project by Francesca Tataranni, “Ancient Rome in Chicago.” This was a virtual tour of classical architecture in Chicago. My video project on the Three Arts Club received particular praise by Professor Tataranni for the research done and the videography and editing skills.
