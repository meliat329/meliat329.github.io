"Drive-Thru" Mask

Date: Fall 2021

Role: Group Member

Course: Design, Thinking, and Doing

Skills: Product Design, User Research, Sewing

Identifying Issues with Masks

For this project my group and I had to create something COVID-19 related. We decided to focus on designing a new mask that would get more people to appropriately wear masks. Our first action was to observe situations where people needed masks. We found that people were less likely to wear a mask properly while exercising.

Brainstorming

We brainstormed many different mask designs that might be able to get athletes to wear a mask properly.

Top Four

We created low-fidelity prototypes of our top four mask ideas.

Testing

We had users test these masks to find which one was the most comfortable and likely to be used properly. We found that the mask with the flap that you can open and close ranked highest among users.

Final Prototype

Finally we created a final prototype of our "Drive-Thru" mask.
