Simple Beginner Unity Games

Date: Fall 2021

Role: Game Designer and Developer

Course: Computer Game Design And Development

Skills: Game Design, Unity, C#

I designed and created these simple 2D Unity Games from scratch for week-long projects in Game Design and Development class. This included finding sprites, using physics simulators, and coding all of the game mechanics.

The first game is a simple platformer where you collect coins and avoid obstacles to get to the finish line. Players use the right and left arrow keys or A and D to move left and right, and the space bar to jump.

The second game is a "fruit catch" style game, but way more cute of course. The player moves the basket left and right to catch the falling kitties, but should avoid the evil dogs. Catching a kittie gives the player one point, while catching a dog causes the player to lose a life. Once the player loses all three lives the game is over and the score is displayed along with the option to restart.
